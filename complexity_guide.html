Recommended Client Progression

Phase 1
Quick Wins
Start with 1-2 quick wins to build trust and demonstrate value. Good openers: Meeting Intelligence, Pre-Call Research, Support Chatbot.
→
Phase 2
Medium Systems
Once trust is established, address core pain. Usually Cold Email, CRM Automation, or Content Systems.
→
Phase 3
Major Systems
Full infrastructure builds after relationship is solid. GodMode often comes after other systems are running.

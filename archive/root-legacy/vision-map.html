SAFETY NETS
Bad day protocol • No sales playbook • Anti-shiny-object rule
▶

Bad Day Protocol

Level 1 (15 min): Post a one-liner. Reply to 3 DMs. Done.
Level 2 (45 min): Repost old content. 5 engagement comments. Check DMs.
Level 3 (2 hrs): Rough content + engagement. Skip outreach.
The rule: You never go to zero. Even Level 1 counts.

No Calls Booked by Day 14?

Diagnose in order: Distribution (are people seeing content?) → Capture (are they downloading?) → Nurture (are emails being opened?) → Guide quality (is the CTA clear?). Fix in that order. Don't change the core strategy.

Anti-Shiny-Object Rule

No new sub-projects until Month 2. The 10 sub-projects above ARE the system. New ideas go in ideas/ folder. Capture without committing.

Accountability

Daily: AI Chief of Staff morning check-in — "Did you post? Did you engage? Did you DM?"
2x Weekly: Maria calls (Tue + Fri, 15-20 min) — "What's working? What's blocking you?"

About Us

The story behind Autom8Lab.

I've been obsessed with technology for as long as I can remember. Not just using it, but understanding how it works and why so many people get left behind. I started in computer science, spent years as a programmer, then moved into marketing. Over a decade in tech, and the thing that never changed was this: I kept meeting smart people who were struggling with tools that should have been making their lives easier.

That's where I found my place. Not on the bleeding edge building things no one understands, but in the middle, translating technology into something accessible. Helping people catch up. Making the complex feel simple.

I started this company because I kept having the same conversation. Business owners telling me they're working 60 hour weeks. That they hired people but still can't delegate. That they've "played with ChatGPT" but don't know how to make it actually useful. They're not behind because they're bad at business. They're behind because the game changed and no one showed them the new rules.

So we built a team that sits at the intersection of development, marketing, and real world business. Developers who understand systems. Marketers who understand people. Experts who've been in the field for years. Together, we help businesses install AI that actually works. Not theory. Not courses. Real systems, running and delivering results.
